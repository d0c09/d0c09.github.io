About



La Chelita is a family-owned restaurant rooted in heritage, community, and resilience. Founded in the 1970s by Graciela "Chelita" Ramírez as a small tortillería llamada La Tapatía, the business quickly became a neighborhood staple known for its handmade tortillas and traditional Jalisco-style dishes. Over the decades, it has remained a symbol of authentic Mexican cooking and a gathering place for local families. Now reimagined as La Chelita, the restaurant continues to honor its origins while evolving to reflect the changing neighborhood and new generations of diners.
